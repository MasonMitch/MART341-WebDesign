How to Cook Pasta

Instructions

Materials Needed

  • Pasta
  • Pot
  • Water
  • Salt

Step 1: Obtain Pasta

You may purchase pasta at a local grocery store if you please. Pick a pasta of your choosing, the kind does not matter.


OR you may make your own pasta. For the purpose of these instructions, we shall be using store bought pasta.

If you wish to make your own pasta here is a video explaining how!

Step 2: Preparing Water

Add 5 quarts of water to a pot.

Add a pinch of salt to the water.

Bring water and salt to a boil.

Step 3: Add Pasta

Once your water is boiling, go ahead and pour your pasta into the water.

Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.

Depending on the pasta you bought or if you made your own, your cooking time may vary!

Step 4: Drain Pasta

Once your pasta is done cooking, grab a strainer and dump your pasta in it and let the water drain.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Congratulations! You have successfully cooked your pasta! Now Enjoy it!
